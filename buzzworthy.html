Summary

Companies focusing on a digital transformation need to think about more than just extending their corporate Intranet. They need to focus on the digital workplace and the various communication and collaboration apps that go into making their employees more productive. Digital workplace platforms exist that help integrate the technology your business uses to help improve productivity by consolidating apps and making them accessible from anywhere. Robin Gareiss discusses the 3 stages of the digital workplace in her blog found on No Jitter.

Who Should Read This Article

Business owners and IT experts looking for ways to make a digital workplace part of their digital transformation.

What You Will Learn

Readers will learn the 3 stages of the digital workplace and general guidelines for each stage. They will also learn about what changes they can expect to see in the final stage. The 3 stages include:

  • Commencement Phase
  • Execution Phase
  • Innovation Phase

Take Away

An important part of a businesses digital transformation is creating a digital workplace that as Gareiss says, "well...works."
