Increase Conversions with the Right Influencer

by Ryman Stringer
publish date 01.16.2017

Whether you are new to influencer marketing or consider yourself a pro, there is a lot of information to navigate through and analyze. The majority of businesses are still trying to figure it out. One of the most overlooked steps in creating an influencer marketing strategy is choosing the right influencer. The influencer you pick directly impacts your website traffic and conversions.

Why use influencer marketing?

Influencers use social media platforms like Twitter or Snapchat to interact and engage with their audience. Influencers are candid and intentional in their posts, videos, and blogs. They share valuable information that helps their audience lead better lives.

Businesses benefit by working with influencers because they get access to a new audience. Since the audience trusts the influencer, they are more apt to take action. The increase in quality website traffic boosts your conversion rates and revenue.

Steps

When you start researching potential influencers to work with, you will find there is a growing number to choose from. Each is unique in how they deliver messages, how they interact with their audience, and what they require from businesses they partner with. The key is to find the best match for your brand values and target audience.

Use these six steps to pick the right influencer for your marketing strategy.

  1. Know your target audience.

It isn’t possible to pick an influencer without knowing who your audience is. Review your existing customer base and poll the top 20% of customers – the ones you want more of. Study the characteristics of these individuals by asking questions. What are the demographics? Who do they follow online? What makes them react – positively and negatively?

  1. Define your goal.

What do you want from influencer marketing? Every business has a different goal. The key is to pick a goal that you can measure and analyze after you go live. Is it to increase your audience reach on social media? Increase your followers? Increase website traffic? Increase sales?

  1. Research influencer options.

Several online vendors like BuzzStream and Grapevine simplify this process by connecting you with potential influencers. Find influencers by industry, audience size, and platform matching your target audience’s characteristics. 

  1. Take a deep look at their values and history.

Once you have a list of possibilities, take a more in-depth look at who they are and what they represent. Review their profiles to determine how often they post, along with their tone and values. Pick a few that match your audience and company values.

  1. Talk to them.

The only way to know if an influencer is the one is to have a conversation. Reach out via e-mail or phone call to connect with them. Choose an influencer that is easy to converse and connect with rather than one with whom the conversation feels forced. This makes building a long-term relationship easier.

  1. Monitor the results.

Review the results after you go live. Make changes where necessary to assure you see a consistent increase in website traffic and conversions.

Choosing the right influencer to represent your business increases your online conversions. Don’t skip these steps in launching your influencer marketing strategy.
